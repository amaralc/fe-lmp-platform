Special Fabrication Processes - PROCESP

DESCRIPTION

The limitation imposed on conventional manufacturing process by new materials leaded to the creation and development of the Special (or Non-conventional) Fabrication Processes.

On the last years, the investigation on this area has raised, always seeking new technologies. With this intention, it was created, in the Precision Engineering Laboratory (LMP), the Special Fabrication Processes Group (PROCESP).

The PROCESP main objective, within the LMP background, is to promote the research in many areas, improving the activities in industry and in research organizations by providing trustworthy data collected on various years of scientific work.

Nowadays, there is being developed works on two research lines, on Electrochemical Erosion area and on Electroerosion by Spark.

On the same background as LMP, the PROCESP is always opened for receiving new ideas and works, which match the industries interests with our research centre interests. Our major objective is to raise the group and institutes know-how.

DEVELOPMENT AREAS

  • MICRO-DRILLING BY ANODIC DISSOLUTION
  • SHARP CORNER WEAR ANALYSIS OF COPPER ELECTRODES ON ELECTROEROSION BY SPARK

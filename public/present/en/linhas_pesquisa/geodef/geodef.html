Defined Geometry Tools Machining - GEODEF

DESCRIPTION

Machining Assays Group lies on the Precision Engineering Laboratory (LMP) since its creation in 1990. From that moment on, works on machining fundaments area with defined geometry tools machining and processes analysis optimization are being developed, in laboratory and factory environments, through partnership with metal-mechanical industries.

In accord with steel, aluminum, and copper manufacture companies, specially, there are made research works that allow us to determine which cutting parameters are ideal on cutting those materials, which material and tool geometry are the most suitable for the job, or how is the performance of new alloys specifically made for machining, in comparison with existing materials. On this kind of work it is possible to analyze developed alloys as well as applied cutting tools. That way interests from different companies on metal-mechanic branch connect themselves in one single research line.

The GEODEF, in all these years of existence, has assisted graduated and post-graduated students of mechanical engineering, supplying the necessary knowledge for its future set on the hard work field.

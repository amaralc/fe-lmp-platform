Fabricação Experimental - EMC5605

SUMMARY

Development of laboratorial works emphasizing optimized machining conditions and cutting tool operational band determination. Works developed on the areas of machining processes with defined geometry cutting tools, non-defined cutting tools, non-conventional machining processes and project and manufacturing of precision and ultraprecision conventional mechanical systems.

PROGRAM

  1. Definition of fabrication process for analysis
  2. Choice of equipments, tools and machine-tools that will be used
  3. Equipments functioning (presentation of fabricants manuals, calibrations procedures, interfacing with computers, study of data acquisition software)
  4. Assays preparation (definition of further assays)
  5. Pre-tests (determination of parameters variation band)
  6. Assays
  7. Results presentation
  8. Seminary defense

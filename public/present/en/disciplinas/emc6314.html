Machining Technology I - EMC6314

SUMMARY

Machining processes technologies that apply cutting tools with defined geometry tools. Empirical study of chip formation mechanisms and wear mechanisms. Presentation of cutting tools materials. Study of cutting fluid influence on machining process. Study of machinability criterions and analysis of many workpieces materials. Economical determination of machining conditions. Machining operations optimization.

PROGRAM

  1. Introduction and presentation of discipline program.
  2. Dimensional precision on fabrication, precision requirement, fabrication geometric errors, roughness, metrology aspects revision, instruments applied on fabrication.
  3. Basement for defined geometry cutting tools – the cutting edge, cutting process, stress on cutting edge, cutting tool wear.
  4. Cutting tools materials and defined geometry cutting tools, historical retrospective of materials applied for cutting tools (metallic materials, carbides, ceramic materials, and hard non-metallic materials), tool configurations, cutting tools standardization, cutting tools preparation, cutting tools cares.
  5. Lubricants and its functions, types, suitable application, influences on machining processes and proper choice.
  6. Machinability and its definition, criterions (tool life, machining forces, surface quality, chip shape), influent factors on machinability, ferrous materials machinability as function of carbon text, influence of other alloy elements on ferrous materials machinability, ferrous materials machinability as function of heat treatment, examples of many ferrous materials machinability, aluminum alloys machinability, copper alloys machinability, nickel alloys machinability, cobalt alloys machinability, titanium alloys machinability, magnesium alloys.
  7. Economical determination of machining conditions, machining conditions optimization, technological limits of cutting conditions, machining processes monitoring and control systems.
  8. Practical applications and case study of machining conditions optimization and machine-tools choice.
  9. Videos.
  10. Seminary presentation.

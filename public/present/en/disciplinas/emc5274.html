Machining Technology with Non-defined Geometry Cutting Tools - EMC5274

SUMMARY

Technology of machining processes that use non-defined geometry tools. Empirical study of chip formation mechanisms and wear mechanisms, presentation of cutting tools materials, binded abrasives fabrication. Study of grinding, burnishing and lapping processes.

PROGRAM

  1. Discipline presentation
  2. Technological basement of non-defined geometry tool cutting process.
  3. Non-defined geometry tools – natural and synthetic abrasive materials, binder materials, wadding materials, fabrication of binded tools, preparation of binded tools, binded tools dressing, profiling and sharpening.
  4. Lubricant fluids.
  5. Grinding with grinding wheels, grinding with abrasive band – basement, technology and applications.
  6. Burnishing – basement, technology and applications.
  7. Lapping – basement, technology and applications.
  8. Slide grinding – basement, technology and applications.
  9. Blasting grinding – basement, technology and applications.
  10. Practical classes of grinding and lapping.

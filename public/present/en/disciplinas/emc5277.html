Special Machining Processes Technology - EMC5277

SUMMARY

Technology of thermal removal by electric discharges processes, chemical machining, thermochemical machining, electrochemical machining, ultra-sound machining, laser machining, electron beam machining and water jet machining. Presentation of the main processes parameters, auxiliary ways and potential applications. Presentation of practical examples for the processes in question.

PROGRAM

  1. Discipline presentation
  2. Electroerosion by electrical discharges – basement, technology and practical applications
  3. Electrode fabrication
  4. Special equipments and processes
  5. Chemical removal – acid attack, thermochemical removal
  6. Electrochemical removal – anodic dissolution principle, processes technologies, special and combined processes, burring and applications
  7. Ultra-sound machining – basement, technology and applications
  8. High power laser ray on fabrication – basement, technology and applications
  9. Electron beam on fabrication – basement, technology and applications
  10. Water jet on fabrication – basement, technology and applications
  11. Practical classes of gaseous electrical discharges machining

History

Since 1983, the Mechanical Engineering Department of UFSC has been giving emphasis in Precision Engineering by improving professor staff know-how and seeking specialized professors in this branch.

In 1986, the precision fabrication division of Machine-tools Laboratory had already a national recognition. That happened when LMP accepted the challenge to develop the first project of an ultraprecision machine-tool, characterizing a new research and develop line on the Mechanical Engineering Department.

With the new structure of the academic formation options of Mechanical Engineering course, LMP, together with the Hardware Laboratory, Metrology Laboratory and the Projects Laboratory, has become the key for the practical formation of the academics of Precision Engineering option.

Nowadays, LMP develops researches on conventional machining aiming at precision fabrication, ultraprecision machining, drilling, precision reaming, tools and processes monitoring, electroerosion, lapping, burnishing, turning with Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN) inserts and project and construction of ultraprecision Machine-Tools. Through accords, there is a close contact with many companies and national and international research laboratories.
